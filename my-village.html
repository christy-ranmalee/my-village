Welcome to My Village – Moratuwa

Welcome to the heart of creativity, culture, and coastal charm. Moratuwa is more than just a suburb of Colombo. it is a place of heritage, music, craftsmanship, natural beauty, and a community with stories to tell. Here, you’ll discover our people, our traditions, our landscapes, and the spirit that makes Moratuwa unique. Thank you for stopping by, come explore, share, and celebrate Moratuwa with us!
